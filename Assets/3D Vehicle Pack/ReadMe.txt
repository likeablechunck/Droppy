How to use 3D Vehicle Pack in your unity game

- Buy 3D Vehicle Pack from Unity Assets Store (http://u3d.as/rJ6)
- Import 3D Vehicle Pack into your unity project.
- You will get 10 vehicles in your project under /Assets/3D Vehicle Pack/Vehicles.
- Just drag and drop any vehicle in your scene and use it.


Example : There is an example scene included with the package.
