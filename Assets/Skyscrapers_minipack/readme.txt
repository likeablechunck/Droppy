Skyscrapers mini pack contains 5 different lowpoly buildings with Diffuse, Normal and Specular textures. 
Textures are tileable with resolutions: 256x256; 256x512; 512x512
*specular maps in the alpha channel of the Diffuse textures

*please note that i changed the shadow distance parameter in the UnityEditor:
(Edit/Project Setting/Quality menu) From 40 to 900 because of the building dimensions (1:1 scale)
